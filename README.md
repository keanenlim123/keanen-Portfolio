#### 👁️ The_Price_You_Pay
> *"Every sin has a consequence."*

A **horror game** built to teach youth the real cost of stealing — through fear, consequence, and confrontation. Three monsters stalk the player, each embodying one of three deadly sins: **Envy**, **Pride**, and **Greed**. The monsters aren't just enemies — they're mirrors.

#### 🚪 Behind_Closed_Doors
> *"The room holds every secret she couldn't say out loud."*

A **VR experience** that walks players into the mind of a daughter silently struggling with mental health. Players explore her bedroom — finding clues, objects, and memories — and must piece together her mental state to **diagnose her condition**. A game about empathy, observation, and what goes unnoticed.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Genre** | VR / Psychological / Narrative |
| **Platform** | VR (PC) |
| **Engine** | Unity |
| **Audience** | General / Mental Health Awareness |
| **Theme** | Mental health, family, what we miss |

#### 🐟 KrispyAnchovies
> *"Clean the ocean. Learn its secrets. Protect what's left."*

An **AR educational game** for children built around ocean conservation. Players clean up habitats in augmented reality, interact with fish species, and discover facts about marine life. Learning disguised as play — because that's when it actually sticks.
